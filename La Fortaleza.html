The Place For Refuge Against Pirates and Natives


La Fortaleza was constructed between 1533-1540, La Fortaleza was authorized by King Carlos for protection from attacks by Indians and pirates. La Fortaleza already has better ones which are like it is already bigger and has more space and many pools. It has served as the governor's residence being the biggest mansion with the most use in America. among the 170 rulers who were in the fortress are John F. Kennedy and his wife, the first lady Jacqueline Kennedy, who visited it in 1961. The King and Queen of Spain Felipe VI and Letizia in 2016, among other politicians, heads of state and members of royalty.

In the actuality, La Fortaleza is the oficial residence of the Puerto Rico governor.
